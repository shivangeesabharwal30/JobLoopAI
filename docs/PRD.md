# JobLoopAI Product Requirements Document

## Product Vision

An AI-powered job search operations agent that automatically monitors Gmail and Google Calendar to maintain a real-time recruiting pipeline and surface recommended next actions.

## User Promise

Connect Google once. Never manually track your job search again.

## Inputs

- Gmail (read-only)
- Google Calendar (read-only)

## Outputs

- Recruiting pipeline
- Action center
- Pipeline health dashboard

## Core Features

1. Detect recruiting-related emails.
2. Identify company and role.
3. Determine recruiting stage.
4. Detect interviews and assignments.
5. Recommend follow-up actions.
6. Surface upcoming deadlines.

## Not Included

- Resume generation
- Interview preparation
- Job matching
- Auto-sending emails
