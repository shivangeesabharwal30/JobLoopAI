# JobLoopAI Dashboard Requirements

## Philosophy

The dashboard should answer:

"What do I need to do right now?"

The dashboard is action-first, not reporting-first.

---

# Dashboard Layout

1. Action Center
2. Upcoming Interviews
3. Active Opportunities
4. Follow-Ups Needed
5. Pipeline Summary
6. Closed Opportunities

---

# Section 1: Action Center

Highest priority section.

Displays tasks requiring attention.

Examples:

- Assignment due in 2 days
- Interview tomorrow
- Follow up with recruiter
- Respond to scheduling request
- Submit references

Fields:

- Task
- Company
- Role
- Due Date
- Priority

Priority Levels:

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

---

# Section 2: Upcoming Interviews

Shows interviews in next 14 days.

Fields:

- Company
- Role
- Interview Type
- Date
- Time

Examples:

Cisco
Senior Product Manager
Hiring Manager Interview

Adobe
Platform Product Manager
Panel Interview

---

# Section 3: Active Opportunities

Shows all active opportunities.

Fields:

- Company
- Role
- Current Stage
- Status
- Last Activity Date

Examples:

Cisco
Senior Product Manager
Hiring Manager Interview

Falcon Labs
AI Operations Specialist
Assignment

---

# Section 4: Follow-Ups Needed

Opportunities requiring outreach.

Rules:

Interview completed > 7 business days ago

OR

Assignment submitted > 7 business days ago

AND

No response detected.

Examples:

Follow up with Cisco

Follow up with Alma

---

# Section 5: Pipeline Summary

Metrics:

Applications Submitted

Active Opportunities

Upcoming Interviews

Offers

Rejections

Dormant Opportunities

---

# Section 6: Closed Opportunities

Includes:

- Rejected
- Withdrawn
- Role Closed

Display separately from active pipeline.

# Dashboard Success Criteria

User can answer in less than 30 seconds:

1. What do I need to do today?
2. What interviews are upcoming?
3. What companies are active?
4. What opportunities need follow-up?
5. Where am I in each recruiting process?
